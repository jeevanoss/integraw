## The challenge

Spice & Co. was running an outdated OpenCart store with slow load times, no mobile optimization, and a checkout process that was losing customers. They needed a modern, fast storefront that could handle both retail and wholesale orders.

## What we built

We migrated the entire product catalogue (1,200+ SKUs) to **PrestaShop 8** and built a custom theme from scratch optimized for Core Web Vitals.

Key features:
- Custom B2B pricing tier system (retail vs. wholesale)
- Razorpay payment integration with UPI, cards, and net banking
- WhatsApp order notification for customers and the fulfillment team
- Multi-language support (English + Malayalam)
- Automated GST invoice generation

## Results

- Lighthouse Performance: **38 → 91**
- Mobile conversion rate: **+67%** in the first 3 months
- Average order value increased by **22%** after redesigning the product pages
- Zero downtime migration from OpenCart
